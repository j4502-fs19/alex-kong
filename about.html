ALex Kong

Coming from China, I am a visual storyteller graduated from Missouri Journalism School. The passion for storytelling drove me across the sea to one of the most renowned journalism schools in the world. As an international student, I am always proud of my identity and my culture. I see the role I am playing in the communication between cultures. Thus, I am passionate in observing the people from various cultural backgrounds, documenting their lives and introducing their voices into the community. Based in school now, I am looking forward to traveling around the country and witness the greatest stories happening around every corner of this land.
